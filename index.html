I'm a Full-Stack Developer based in Basildon, UK, with just over three years of hands-on software engineering experience — primarily in the MERN stack and AWS — and nearly a year inside the executive operations of a UK pharmaceutical PLC.

My most formative work happened at Nuvepro Technologies in Bengaluru, where I owned the Lab Administrator Portal end-to-end: architecture, RESTful APIs, MongoDB schemas, Jest tests, Dockerised deployment to AWS EC2 — and mentored two junior developers along the way.

I came to the UK in 2023 on the back of two competitive scholarships — the Commonwealth Postgraduate Bursary and the Vice-Chancellor's International Attainment Scholarship — and graduated from the University of Liverpool with Merit Honours in 2024.

The slightly unusual part of my profile: at Waymade PLC I started as an Executive Assistant and grew the role into something hybrid — I now build internal React/TypeScript tooling, run a Monday-to-Power-BI migration over GraphQL, and prototype AI-assisted workflow automation alongside my admin work. As of May 2026 I am also a part-time Project Manager at Blackmont Consulting, leading a team of seven Business Analysts.

That hybrid — production engineering + executive stakeholder exposure + regulated-industry awareness — is what I'd bring to a product team.
